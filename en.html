Meet Balakhilya das

I came to this holistic yoga philosophy in an indirect way. I began to be interested in hatha yoga (physical yoga) as a way to improve my surfing, which at that time (1969) was my whole life. The benefits were immediately obvious, and I started looking into the subject. By reading, I began to practice simple meditation, ate vegetarian food, and spent more and more time in deeper contemplation on the meaning of my life. I had always been a traveler, but now I started an inner journey. Along the way, my world, both the outer and the inner, became more peaceful and calm.

In 1971, I was so incredibly fortunate (not by coincidence, I'm sure) to get in touch with my spiritual teachers. Under their expertise and guidance, I received and began to follow the ancient science called Bhakti Yoga, which I still do to this day.

And what a beautiful and satisfying journey it has been!

So I strongly wish you can get the same chance and blessing that I have had, undeserving as I am.

Best wishes,

Balakhilya das

Reincarnation

Have I been here before?

The phenomenon of reincarnation (or transmigration of the soul) is a subject that is relevant to each and every one of us who ever has asked the question 'How did I come into this world?' and 'Is there life after death?'.

The subject of transmigration of the soul has been discussed in cinema, science and literature, and there are lots of popular entertainment programs about reincarnation - with evidence and arguments supporting this phenomenon as well as denying it.

However, despite all this, very few people really understand the true mechanism of the reincarnation process. Who reincarnates? What laws govern the process? How many lives do we have to live? Can one be born in the next life as an animal? Do plants have a soul? Can you remember your past lives? Is there scientific evidence of reincarnation? 

These and many other questions will be considered at an open lecture of Balakhilya das on 'Reincarnation'. In addition, you will have the opportunity to learn simple and effective techniques of meditation with mantras.

Life Without Anxiety

Come to find inner peace & purpose in your life

"You cannot avoid stress because stress is everywhere. And the more we've advanced in our modern technology and ways to enjoy the world, the more we have to work - people are desperately trying to find happiness. We’re under great pressure. To be successful, to be happy, and to make money to support our more extravagant lifestyles. And we’ve been caught in a whirlwind.

And one of the most stressful things is: you’re threatened with death. So material life is stressful. So the solution is not a material solution. Moving to an island in the middle of the Pacific ocean is not going to solve the problem. Then you’ll be stressed about "I’m lonely!", “Where am I going to get something to eat?” or “Oh, what am I going to do? I’m going to die here… alone?”.

So really there’s only one solution and it’s a spiritual solution. If I cultivate spiritual love and happiness in my heart and I’m actively living a balanced life, then I’m peaceful inside, I’m calm inside. And so the whirlwind can be flying all around me, there’s stress everywhere; job stress, family stress, pollution from the sound, the food, the air, etc. You know, the hurricane is tearing everything to pieces around us, but in the eye of the storm everything is calm and peaceful. So this is the only solution."

Balakhilya das
